Music: trombone, clarinet, piano

I played the clarinet and trombone throughout high school, performing in our orchestra and jazz band. Despite the gruelling rehearsal hours (7 A.M. band practise twice a week for the orchestra), these ensembles were my favourite part of high school. Some of my fondest memories come from our band's visit to Europe and the soloes I performed in Toronto's jazz bar, The Rex. Aside from these instruments, I also have many years of piano experience, and I enjoy playing the soundtracks to my favourite shows on the keyboard.
